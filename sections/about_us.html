About us

Since our modest beginnings in 2018 with a very small space in the charming neighborhood of Tlalpan, "Pasta & Dining" has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Italian-roused homestyle food. Today, we are still consistent delivering this unique experience and vision. We source our produce from local farmers, as we believe that only the best ingredients belong on your plate. Our menu offers something for everybody, from gorgeous plates of mixed greens accompanied with our in-house dressing to an extraordinary slice of pizza. These are baked in-house to guarantee flawlessness and expertly baked in our stone oven at over 400 degrees, ensuring a crispy bottom and a crunchy yet airy crust. We trust that whether you’re a laid back high-school skater or occupied businessman, you will walk into "Pasta & Dining", enjoy the good music, beautiful aromas and state to yourself, “Ahh… this is exactly where I need to be.”
